ABOUT KASHMIRI CHAI:

Kashmiri Chai, also known as Noon Chai, is a traditional tea from Kashmir, known for its unique pink color and creamy texture. Made with green tea leaves, milk, and a special blend of spices like cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves, this tea is both flavorful and soothing. Often garnished with crushed nuts, such as almonds or pistachios, Kashmiri Chai is slightly salty, giving it a distinctive savory twist compared to traditional teas.
At I's Restaurant, our Kashmiri Chai is carefully prepared, offering a rich, creamy, and aromatic experience with every sip. It's the perfect drink for those who enjoy a comforting and exotic tea experience, especially when paired with traditional snacks!
