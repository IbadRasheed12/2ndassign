ABOUT ELAICHI CHAI:

Elaichi Chai, also known as Cardamom Tea, is a flavorful and aromatic tea infused with the warm, spicy notes of cardamom. Made by brewing tea leaves with milk and crushed cardamom pods, it offers a sweet and fragrant flavor that is both comforting and invigorating. The subtle spiciness of the cardamom pairs beautifully with the rich, creamy tea base, creating a perfect balance of taste.
At I's Restaurant, our Elaichi Chai is prepared fresh with high-quality cardamom, delivering a fragrant and soothing tea experience that is perfect for any time of the day. It's the ideal beverage for those who appreciate the delicate flavor of cardamom in their tea!
