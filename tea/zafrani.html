ABOUT ZAFRANI CHAI:

Zafrani Chai is a luxurious and aromatic tea made with a blend of high-quality tea leaves, milk, and the rich essence of saffron (zafran). Infused with a delicate mix of spices such as cardamom and cinnamon, this tea offers a soothing, fragrant experience with a golden hue that is both refreshing and indulgent.
At I's Restaurant, our Zafrani Chai is prepared with the finest saffron, delivering a rich, warm, and aromatic flavor with every sip. It's a perfect drink for those who enjoy a fragrant, premium tea experience, ideal for relaxing moments or special occasions.
