ABOUT VANILLA ICECREAM:

Vanilla Ice Cream is a classic and beloved dessert known for its smooth, creamy texture and rich vanilla flavor. Made with high-quality cream, milk, and real vanilla extract, it delivers a refreshing and indulgent taste that’s both sweet and subtle.
At I's Restaurant, our Vanilla Ice Cream is made with the finest ingredients, offering a velvety consistency and a perfectly balanced vanilla flavor in every scoop. It’s the perfect treat on its own or paired with toppings like chocolate syrup, fruits, or sprinkles!
