ABOUT MANGO ICECREAM:

Mango Ice Cream is a tropical delight that combines the rich, sweet flavor of ripe mangoes with a creamy, smooth texture. Made with fresh mango pulp, cream, and sugar, this ice cream delivers a refreshing and indulgent taste of summer in every scoop.
At I's Restaurant, our Mango Ice Cream is crafted with the finest, fresh mangoes, ensuring a natural, fruity flavor that’s both vibrant and sweet. It’s the perfect treat for mango lovers, offering a refreshing escape from the heat or a delicious ending to any meal!
