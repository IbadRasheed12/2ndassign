ABOUT BEEF PULAO

Beef Pulao is a hearty and flavorful rice dish, featuring tender beef cooked in a fragrant broth of spices and basmati rice. The beef is simmered to perfection with whole spices like cinnamon, cardamom, and cloves, creating a rich and aromatic flavor that infuses every grain of rice.
At I's Restaurant, our Beef Pulao is crafted with premium ingredients and traditional techniques, making it a comforting and satisfying choice for anyone craving a delicious and wholesome meal.
