ABOUT WHITE KHADAI:

White Karahi is a creamy and mildly spiced variation of the traditional karahi, offering a unique and flavorful experience. Made with tender chicken or mutton, it features a base of yogurt, cream, garlic, and ginger, without the use of tomatoes or red chili. This gives it a rich, creamy texture and a subtle yet delicious taste. Garnished with green chilies, black pepper, and fresh coriander, White Karahi pairs perfectly with naan or paratha.
At I's Restaurant, our White Karahi is a customer favorite, prepared with authentic ingredients for a smooth and flavorful dining experience!
