ABOUT MUTTON KHADAI:

Mutton Karahi is a classic and indulgent dish from Pakistani cuisine, known for its rich, spicy, and savory flavors. Tender mutton pieces are slow-cooked in a wok (karahi) with a base of fresh tomatoes, ginger, garlic, green chilies, and a blend of traditional spices, creating a thick and flavorful gravy. Garnished with fresh coriander and sliced ginger, Mutton Karahi is best enjoyed with hot naan or steamed rice.
At I's Restaurant, our Mutton Karahi is crafted with care, delivering the perfect balance of tenderness and spice for a truly memorable meal!
