ABOUT CHICKEN KHADAI:

Chicken Karahi is a traditional and flavorful Pakistani dish, known for its rich, spicy, and aromatic taste. It is made with tender chicken pieces cooked in a wok (karahi) with a blend of tomatoes, garlic, ginger, green chilies, and a variety of authentic spices. Often garnished with fresh coriander and julienned ginger, Chicken Karahi is a dish that pairs perfectly with naan or steamed rice.
At I's Restaurant, our Chicken Karahi is prepared with fresh ingredients and traditional techniques, ensuring a delicious and satisfying experience for every diner!
