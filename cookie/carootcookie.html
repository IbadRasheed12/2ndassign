ABOUT CAROOT CAKE COOKIE:

Carrot Cake Cookie is a unique and delicious twist on the classic carrot cake, transformed into a soft, chewy cookie. Made with grated carrots, warm spices like cinnamon and nutmeg, and a touch of cream cheese, these cookies capture the essence of carrot cake in every bite.
At I's Restaurant, our Carrot Cake Cookies are baked to perfection, offering a moist and flavorful texture with a hint of sweetness and spice. Topped with a light cream cheese drizzle, these cookies are a delightful treat for those who love the comforting taste of carrot cake in a portable form!
