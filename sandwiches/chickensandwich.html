ABOUT CHICKEN SANDWICH:

A Chicken Sandwich is a delightful and versatile dish, perfect for any occasion. It features tender, juicy chicken, seasoned to perfection, and served between soft, toasted bread. Whether grilled, fried, or in a crispy patty, the chicken is complemented with fresh lettuce, tomatoes, pickles, and a choice of sauces like mayonnaise, mustard, or BBQ.
At I's Restaurant, our Chicken Sandwich is a customer favorite, offering a delicious combination of savory, fresh, and satisfying flavors in every bite. It's the perfect meal for a quick lunch or a comforting dinner!
