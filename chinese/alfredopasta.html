ABOUT ALFREDO PASTA:

Alfredo Pasta is a creamy and indulgent Italian-inspired dish, loved for its rich, velvety texture and comforting flavors. Made with tender pasta tossed in a luscious Alfredo sauce crafted from butter, cream, and Parmesan cheese, it’s a true delight for cheese lovers. Often paired with grilled chicken, sautéed mushrooms, or fresh parsley, Alfredo Pasta is a perfect balance of creamy goodness and hearty satisfaction.
At I's Restaurant, our Alfredo Pasta is prepared fresh, ensuring every bite is smooth, flavorful, and unforgettable!
