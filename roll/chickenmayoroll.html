ABOUT CHICKEN MAYO ROLL

A Chicken Mayo Roll is a delicious and creamy snack that features tender, grilled or fried chicken wrapped in soft, warm flatbread or naan. The chicken is mixed with a rich and smooth mayonnaise dressing, along with fresh vegetables like lettuce, cucumbers, and tomatoes, providing a perfect combination of creamy, crunchy, and savory flavors.
At I's Restaurant, our Chicken Mayo Roll is made with juicy, seasoned chicken and a generous spread of creamy mayo, creating a satisfying and flavorful meal that's perfect for a quick bite or a tasty lunch on the go!
