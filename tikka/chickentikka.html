ABOUT CHICKEN TIKKA

Chicken Tikka is a popular and flavorful dish made from boneless chicken marinated in a blend of yogurt, spices, and herbs. The chicken is then skewered and grilled, creating a smoky, charred flavor on the outside while keeping the meat tender and juicy on the inside.
At I's Restaurant, our Chicken Tikka is perfectly spiced, offering a balance of heat, tang, and smokiness. It’s served with naan, raita, or a tangy chutney, making it a delicious and satisfying choice for any meal. Whether you're a fan of mild or spicy flavors, our Chicken Tikka delivers a delightful burst of taste with every bite!
