ABOUT GREEN TIKKA:

Green Tikka is a flavorful variation of the traditional tikka, made by marinating chicken or beef in a vibrant green mixture of fresh herbs, spices, yogurt, and mint. This unique marinade gives the meat a fresh, aromatic flavor with a mild spiciness and a refreshing herbal taste. Grilled to perfection, Green Tikka offers a tender and juicy bite with a smoky, charred exterior.
