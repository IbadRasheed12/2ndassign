ABOUT BIHARI TIKKA:

Bihari Tikka is a flavorful and aromatic dish made from marinated chicken or beef pieces, infused with a special blend of Bihari spices, yogurt, and herbs. The marination process ensures that the meat absorbs all the spices, resulting in tender, juicy, and flavorful pieces of meat. Grilled to perfection, Bihari Tikka has a smoky, charred exterior with a tender and juicy interior.
At I's Restaurant, our Bihari Tikka is made with authentic Bihari recipes, offering a deliciously spiced, melt-in-your-mouth experience that's perfect for those who enjoy bold, aromatic flavors. It’s a popular choice for BBQ lovers and pairs wonderfully with naan or rice!
