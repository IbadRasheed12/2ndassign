ABOUT BBQ TIKKA PIZZA:

BBQ Tikka Pizza is a mouthwatering fusion of smoky barbecue flavors and spicy tikka goodness, all atop a delicious pizza crust. Topped with tender tikka-marinated chicken chunks, fresh onions, bell peppers, and a tangy barbecue sauce, this pizza delivers bold and satisfying flavors in every bite. The melted mozzarella cheese ties it all together, creating the perfect blend of spice, smoke, and creaminess.
At I's Restaurant, our BBQ Tikka Pizza is a fan favorite for its unique combination of desi flavors and classic pizza style!
