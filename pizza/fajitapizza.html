ABOUT FAJITA PIZZA:

Chicken Fajita Pizza is a delicious fusion of spicy fajita flavors and cheesy pizza goodness. It’s topped with tender, marinated chicken pieces, fresh bell peppers, onions, and a zesty fajita seasoning that packs a flavorful punch. Layered on a crispy or soft crust with a generous spread of tangy pizza sauce and melted mozzarella cheese, this pizza is a feast for both spice and cheese lovers.
At I's Restaurant, our Chicken Fajita Pizza is a customer favorite, delivering the perfect combination of bold flavors and irresistible freshness!
