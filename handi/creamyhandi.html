ABOUT CREAMY HANDI

Creamy Handi is a rich and indulgent dish made with tender chicken pieces simmered in a smooth, creamy gravy. The base is created using yogurt, fresh cream, and a blend of aromatic spices, which results in a velvety texture and a deliciously mild flavor. Cooked in a traditional handi (clay pot), the dish is infused with the essence of slow-cooked spices, giving it a comforting, creamy taste that pairs beautifully with naan or rice.
At I's Restaurant, our Creamy Handi is a perfect choice for those who love rich, flavorful curries with a smooth and luxurious texture!
