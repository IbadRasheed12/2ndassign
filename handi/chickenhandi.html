ABOUT CHICKEN HANDI:

Chicken Handi is a rich and flavorful dish, known for its creamy texture and aromatic spices. Made with tender chicken pieces, this dish is cooked in a traditional clay pot (handi) with a blend of yogurt, cream, tomatoes, ginger, garlic, and a variety of spices that infuse the chicken with incredible depth of flavor. Slow-cooked to perfection, Chicken Handi is often garnished with fresh coriander and served hot, making it the perfect comfort food.
At I's Restaurant, our Chicken Handi is crafted with care and the finest ingredients, delivering a warm, comforting, and indulgent experience in every bite!
