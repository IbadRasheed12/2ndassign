ABOUT RABRI FALOODA:

Rabri Falooda is a decadent, sweet dessert that combines the richness of rabri (a traditional Indian dessert made with condensed milk and flavored with cardamom) with the refreshing, fruity textures of falooda. This dessert features layers of rose syrup, basil seeds, jelly, and vermicelli noodles, topped with a generous scoop of rabri and ice cream.
