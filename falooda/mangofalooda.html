ABOUT MANGO FALOODA:

Mango Falooda is a tropical, refreshing dessert that combines the rich sweetness of fresh mangoes with the delightful textures of falooda. This dessert features layers of mango puree, vermicelli noodles, basil seeds, rose syrup, and jelly, topped with a generous scoop of vanilla or mango ice cream.
At I's Restaurant, our Mango Falooda is made with the finest, ripest mangoes, creating a luscious, creamy treat that’s both refreshing and indulgent. It’s the perfect dessert for mango lovers, offering a delicious balance of fruity flavors and delightful textures!
