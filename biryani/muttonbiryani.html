ABOUT BEEF BIRYANI:

Mutton Biryani is a luxurious and flavorful dish, featuring tender, slow-cooked mutton infused with aromatic spices and layered with fragrant basmati rice. Each layer is packed with rich masala, juicy meat, and the perfect balance of spices, creating a dish that is both hearty and satisfying. Topped with fried onions, fresh herbs, and a hint of saffron, Mutton Biryani is a feast for the senses.
At I's Restaurant, our Mutton Biryani is prepared with authentic recipes and premium ingredients, making it a must-try for biryani lovers craving traditional, bold flavors!
