ABOUT BEEF BIRYANI:

Beef Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic dish that combines tender, slow-cooked beef with fragrant basmati rice, infused with a blend of traditional spices. Layers of juicy beef, spicy masala, and perfectly cooked rice are topped with fried onions, fresh herbs, and a hint of saffron for a rich and satisfying taste.
At I's Restaurant, our Beef Biryani is prepared with authentic spices and techniques, delivering the perfect balance of heat, aroma, and flavor. It’s a must-try for anyone who loves hearty and traditional desi cuisine!
