ABOUT CHICKEN BOTI:

Chicken Boti is a flavorful and tender dish made with marinated chicken pieces, grilled or cooked to perfection. The chicken is marinated in a blend of spices, yogurt, and herbs, allowing the flavors to infuse deeply, creating a juicy and aromatic dish. Often served with naan or rice, it is a popular choice for those who enjoy smoky, spicy, and tender chicken.
At I's Restaurant, our Chicken Boti is prepared using traditional recipes, ensuring every bite is packed with bold flavors and a satisfying texture. It's perfect for anyone craving a delicious and savory chicken dish with a hint of spice!
