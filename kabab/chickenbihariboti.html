ABOUT CHICKEN BIHARI BOTI:

Chicken Bihari Boti is a tender and flavorful dish made with marinated chicken pieces, infused with the aromatic spices and herbs of traditional Bihari cuisine. The chicken is marinated in a special blend of yogurt, ginger, garlic, and a mix of spices, allowing the flavors to penetrate deeply, making it juicy and aromatic. Grilled to perfection, the chicken is tender on the inside with a smoky, charred exterior, making it an irresistible dish.
At I's Restaurant, our Chicken Bihari Boti is prepared using authentic recipes, offering a perfect balance of spice, tenderness, and flavor, ideal for those who crave a delicious and spicy grilled chicken experience!
