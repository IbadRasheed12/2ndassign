ABOUT BEEF BIHARI BOTI:

Beef Bihari Boti is a mouthwatering and flavorful dish made with tender beef pieces marinated in a special blend of spices, yogurt, and herbs, inspired by the traditional Bihari cuisine. The marinade, which includes ingredients like ginger, garlic, and a variety of aromatic spices, infuses the beef with rich flavors, making it incredibly juicy and tender. Grilled to perfection, the Beef Bihari Boti is served sizzling hot, often with naan or rice, and garnished with fresh herbs.
At I's Restaurant, our Beef Bihari Boti is crafted with the finest beef and authentic seasonings, offering a delicious and smoky experience in every bite!
