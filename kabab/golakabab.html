ABOUT GOLA KABAB:

Gola Kabab is a traditional and flavorful grilled dish made from minced meat (usually beef or chicken) mixed with spices, herbs, and onions, then shaped into round balls. These meatballs are grilled to perfection, offering a juicy, smoky flavor with a crispy exterior. Gola Kabab is typically served with naan, raita, or chutney and is a popular choice for BBQ lovers.
At I's Restaurant, our Gola Kabab is made with the finest ingredients and grilled to perfection, offering a mouthwatering combination of flavors that’s sure to satisfy your craving for tender, spiced meat!
